Error codes of the call invitation methods.

0: The method call succeeds.

1: The method call fails. Invalid argument.

2: The method call fails. The call invitation has not started.

3: The method call fails. The call invitation has ended.

4: The method call fails. The call invitation is already accepted.

5: The method call fails. The call invitation is already sent.

typedef NS_ENUM ( NSInteger  ,
AgoraRtmLocalInvitationErrorCode   
)

RETURNED TO THE CALLER.

Error codes of an outgoing call invitation.

0: RETURNED TO THE CALLER. The outgoing call invitation succeeds.

1: RETURNED TO THE CALLER. The callee is offline.

The SDK:

  • Keeps resending the call invitation to the callee, if he or she is offline.
  • Returns this error code, if he or she is still offline 30 seconds since the call invitation is sent.

2: RETURNED TO THE CALLER. The callee is online but has not ACKed to the call invitation 30 seconds since it is sent.

3: RETURNED TO THE CALLER. SAVED FOR FUTURE USE. The call invitation expires 60 seconds since it is sent, if the callee ACKs to the call invitation but neither the caller or callee takes any further action (cancel, accpet, or decline it).

4: RETURNED TO THE CALLER. The caller is not logged in.

RETURNED TO THE CALLER.

States of an outgoing call invitation.

0: RETURNED TO THE CALLER. The initial state of a call invitation (idle).

1: FOR INTERNAL USE ONLY.

2: RETURNED TO THE CALLER. The call invitation is received by the callee.

3: RETURNED TO THE CALLER. The call invitation is accepted by the callee.

4: RETURNED TO THE CALLER. The call invitation is declined by the callee.

5: RETURNED TO THE CALLER. You have canceled the call invitation.

6: RETURNED TO THE CALLER. The life cycle of the outgoing call invitation ends in failure.

AgoraRtmRemoteInvitationErrorCode   
)

RETURNED TO THE CALLEE.

Error codes of an incoming call invitation.

0: RETURNED TO THE CALLEE. The incoming call invitation succeeds.

1: RETURNED TO THE CALLEE. The call invitation received by the callee fails: the callee is not online.

2: RETURNED TO THE CALLEE. The call invitation received by callee fails: the acceptance of the call invitation fails.

3: RETURNED TO THE CALLEE. The call invitation received by the callee fails: the call invitation expires 60 seconds since it is sent, if the callee ACKs to the call invitation but neither the caller or callee takes any further action (cancel, accpet, or decline it).

RETURNED TO THE CALLEE.

States of an incoming call invitation.

0: RETURNED TO THE CALLEE. The initial state of a call invitation (idle).

1: RETURNED TO THE CALLEE. A call invitation from a remote caller is received.

2: FOR INTERNAL USE ONLY.

3: RETURNED TO THE CALLEE. You have declined the call invitation.

4: RETURNED TO THE CALLEE. You have accepted the call invitation.

5: RETURNED TO THE CALLEE. The call invitation is canceled by the remote caller.

6: RETURNED TO THE CALLEE. The life cycle of the incoming call invitation ends in failure.

void acceptRemoteInvitation

Allows the callee to accept a call invitation.

Parameters
remoteInvitationAn AgoraRtmRemoteInvitation object.
completionAn AgoraRtmRemoteInvitationAcceptBlock object.
  • Success:
    • The caller receives the AgoraRtmRemoteInvitationAcceptBlock object with the AgoraRtmInvitationApiCallErrorOk state and the [localInvitationAccepted]([AgoraRtmCallDelegate rtmCallKit:localInvitationAccepted:withResponse:]) callback.
    • The callee receives the [remoteInvitationAccepted]([AgoraRtmCallDelegate rtmCallKit:remoteInvitationAccepted:]) callback.
  • Failure: The caller receives the AgoraRtmRemoteInvitationAcceptBlock object with an error code. See AgoraRtmInvitationApiCallErrorCode for the error codes.
